ABOUT US — CEO GREETING
A Message from Our CEO
Dear customers and partners worldwide,
The semiconductor industry demands ever-tighter tolerances and ever-higher throughput from every component in the process chain. At Saehan Nanotech, we focus on solving the machining challenges at the front of that chain — the precision-drilled consumable components that keep etch, CVD, and ALD chambers running.
Our patented Both-Side Spindle Drilling Machine (ANT-BSSD500) was born from a direct observation: the workpiece reversal step in conventional electrode drilling is the single largest source of concentricity defects. By drilling both faces simultaneously, we eliminate that error at its source. The result is a machine that consistently holds 20μm concentricity — with 2-3× the throughput of vertical alternatives.
Alongside our drilling technology, our multi-wire and single-wire diamond wire saws give customers a complete solution for slicing hard brittle semiconductor materials — from monocrystalline silicon to SiC, sapphire, quartz, and ALN — with industry-leading kerf loss performance.
Every machine we ship carries a commitment: if it leaves our factory, it meets spec. We back that with hands-on training and responsive after-sales service, because we know that downtime in semiconductor manufacturing is measured in dollars per minute.
We look forward to being your precision machining partner.
Ahn Kook JinCEO & Founder, Saehan Nanotech Co., Ltd.

DIFFERENTIATION
Two Engineering Innovations That Redefine Hole Drilling
Both-side simultaneous drilling and a horizontal architecture lift quality, machinability, and throughput at the same time.
01
Both-Side Simultaneous Drilling
Eliminates the workpiece-flip step — the largest source of concentricity error in conventional drilling.
BEFORE
Single-Direction Type
Drill one face, then flip and re-fixture for a 2-step process
Pitch error and concentricity defects introduced during the flip
Total cycle time grows; operator intervention raises labor cost
Alignment error reduces yield
AFTER
Both-Side Type
Two spindles drill simultaneously from both faces — no flip required
Concentricity held within 20㎛ at all times
Hole quality preserved even when machine pitch error occurs
Longer unattended runs — higher yield, more output
02
Horizontal Machining Structure
A horizontal hole axis lets chips evacuate naturally — the key enabler for true deep-hole drilling.
BEFORE
Vertical Type
Gravity packs swarf inside the hole
Reduced coolant flow — risk of drill breakage
Hard ceiling on deep-hole capability
AFTER
Horizontal Type
Chips evacuate horizontally by gravity — drill protected
1.5–2× throughput at identical cutting conditions
2–3× throughput with optimized recipe

Q01
What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
We do not have a fixed MOQ. Every machine is custom-designed and manufactured to your specific requirements, so we accept orders starting from a single unit. For consumables such as diamond loop wire, please contact us for quantity pricing.
Q02
What is the typical lead time for a machine?
Lead time depends on the machine type and customization level. Standard configurations typically take 3–6 months from order confirmation to delivery. Highly customized machines or new designs may require additional development time. We provide a detailed timeline at the quotation stage.
Q03
Do you provide after-sales service internationally?
Yes. We provide after-sales service and technical support to all export customers. Our engineers can provide remote troubleshooting, on-site maintenance visits, and spare parts delivery worldwide. We have experience servicing machines in the USA, China, Malaysia, and Vietnam.
Q04
What materials can your machines process?
Our machines are designed for hard and brittle materials including monocrystalline and polycrystalline silicon, SiC (silicon carbide), sapphire, quartz (fused silica), AlN (aluminum nitride), alumina ceramic, graphite, optical glass, and rare earth permanent magnets (NdFeB, SmCo). Each machine is optimized for specific material characteristics.
Q05
Can you customize a machine for my specific process?
Absolutely. Customization is our core strength. We design every machine based on your material type, workpiece geometry, required tolerances, and production volume. From the mechanical structure and spindle configuration to the CNC controller and coolant system — everything is tailored to your process.
Q06
Do you offer machining test services?
Yes. We can perform cutting, grinding, or drilling tests on your material samples at our Changwon factory before you commit to a machine purchase. This allows us to validate process parameters and demonstrate achievable quality. Contact us to arrange a test.

What countries do you export to?
Our equipment serves diverse industries including semiconductor manufacturing, rare earth permanent magnet processing, display production, and defense applications.
