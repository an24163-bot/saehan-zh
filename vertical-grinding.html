Vertical Grinding Machine（立式磨削机）

ANT-VGM 600

Vertical Cylindrical Grinding Machine

High-rigidity combined vertical grinding machine that completes ID, OD, and face machining of SiC, Si, and Ceramic Ring materials in a single setup.

  • 01High-rigidity structural design suppresses vibration for long-duration stable machining
  • 02Ring material ID, OD, and face — 3-surface simultaneous machining reduces process steps
  • 03Optimized high-precision grinding for SiC, Si, and Ceramic hard materials
Ø 600 mmLarge-Area Table
6.3 kWHigh-Efficiency Inverter Motor
FANUC0iMF-P Controller
3-SIDE SIMULTANEOUS GRINDING

All Three Surfaces Finished in a Single Setup

OD, face, and ID grinding are completed back-to-back on one machine — cutting cycle time and eliminating the dimensional drift that comes with re-fixturing.

01

OD Grinding

External Diameter

A grinding wheel precision-finishes the outer circumference of the ring. Roundness and outer diameter dimension are held simultaneously.

02

Face Grinding

Top Surface

Surface-grinds the top face for uniform thickness and parallelism — meeting the flatness requirements of downstream processes.

03

ID Grinding

Inner Diameter

Grinds the central bore of the ring. Holds tight ID dimensional tolerance while maintaining ID/OD concentricity in a single chucking.

KEY FEATURES

Six Engineering Highlights at a Glance

The mechanical and control choices that make the ANT-VGM600 a precision-grinding workhorse for hard, brittle ring materials.

01

3-Side Simultaneous Machining

All grinding faces — OD, face, and ID — are completed in a single setup. No re-fixturing means no re-introduced tolerance error.

02

Ø600 Large-Format Table

Table diameter Ø600 mm, supports Max Ø600 × Max 100 L workpieces. Standard Ø550 vacuum jig reliably holds large rings during high-load grinding.

03

FANUC 0iMF-P CNC

Industry-standard FANUC 0iMF-P controller delivers high reliability and serviceable maintenance. X- and Z-axis AC servo drives deliver precision feed.

04

6.3 kW High-Efficiency Inverter Motor

Spindle and rotary table use 6.3 kW high-efficiency inverter motors. Spindle 1,350 RPM (inverter), table 0–200 RPM (servo) control.

05

Resin / Vitrified Diamond Wheels

Standard Metal Diamond Wheel (OD Ø250); resin- and vitrified-bonded diamond wheels available for high-precision grinding of SiC, Si, ceramic hard brittle materials.

06

250 ℓ High-Volume Coolant System

250 ℓ coolant tank with 60 ℓ/min pump — high-volume cutting-fluid delivery maximizes grinding-heat removal and chip flushing.
